Welcome to the Cozy Mystery Road Trip!

Grab your favorite blanket and a hot cup of something delicious — we're traveling the United States, one book at a time. We've mapped out a suggested itinerary below, state by state, but feel free to chart your own course, whether you're reading solo or alongside a group of friends.

Each week we'll pull into a new state, with a featured book to guide the way and a handful of other local reads to explore if you want to linger a little longer.

However you travel, we'd love to see your stops along the way — share them with #CozyMysteryRoadTrip
